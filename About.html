Saving Water for Our Children

Water is becoming more and more precious to our planet. This application will focus on the worldwide problem of water shortages. Some daily activities from all of us, including our children, will lead to water depletion. What we will do in this solution is to create awareness of children about this water shortages problem.

Our application targets on young children and inform them about the water shortage problem. To convey this idea, our app will include some interesting games, videos, pictures, animations, graphics, and articles related to this topic.
